How it works

One app. Everything connected.

01

Browse normally

YANTRA is a full Chromium browser. Open any site, log in, navigate — just like Chrome. The AI watches context but never sends data without you asking.

02

Ask in natural language

Press ⌘K to open the command palette or click the chat panel. Ask anything about the page, the web, or your memory.

03

Automate what repeats

Turn any interaction into a routine. Set a schedule or a condition trigger. YANTRA runs it autonomously and posts results to your activity feed.

04

Everything stays local

Your credentials, memory, and chat history live in ~/.yantra/ on your Mac. No account required. No data leaves your machine without your consent.
